Chicken & Risotto

Homepage

A staple meal in my home, simple and all in one pot that produces tasty chicken and decadent risotto.

Ingredients:

*Note: Ingredient quantities are approximate, I suggest trying to match the amounts the first time you try the recipe then adjust to your liking thereafter

    Optional:

  • Parsley (for garnish)
  • Chicken Drumsticks can also be used
  • Chicken Bouillon Cube

    • Required:

  • 9 Pieces Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Large Red Onion, chopped
  • 3-6 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cooking Fat (e.g. Olive Oil, Ghee, Schmaltz)
  • Chicken Stock or Broth (Homemade is best)
  • 1.5 cups Brown Rice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F. Heat a dutch oven or heavy pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Season your chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika on both sides.
  3. Brown the chicken with the pot in batches, start skin side down. Do not crowd the pot as this will cause the chicken to boil instead of searing.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside, add the onions to the pot and stir occasionally.
  5. Once the onions are softened (approximately 2 minutes), add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  6. Add the brown rice to the pot and stir over the heat for about 30 seconds.
  7. Add 1 cup of chicken stock and stir to remove any brown bits from searing the chicken.
  8. Add the chicken back into the pot and add the stock until the chicken is covered. Optionally add a buillon cube for extra chicken flavor.
  9. Add the lid to the pot (slightly ajar) and put in the oven for 40 minutes.
  10. After 40 mintues remove the pot from the oven, if the rice is not finished, simmer over medium-low heat until cooked.
  11. Serve the delicious chicken and risotto together with chopped garnish on top such as flat leafed parsley!
